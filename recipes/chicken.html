Kung Pao Chicken

Kung Pao Chicken Description

Kung Pao Chicken consists of diced, marinated chicken, stir-fried with orange or orange juice, ginger, garlic, chicken broth, sugar, cooking oil, corn starch, and salt and pepper to taste. Many other vegetables may be added, such as onion, bell pepper or carrots.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Hoisin Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Hot Dried Peppers
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Heat the Vegetable Oil in a Wok or Pan until smoking
  2. Fry the Hot Dried Peppers in the Oil until fragrent, then remove
  3. Stir Fry the onions and bell peppers until soft
  4. Stir Fry the chicken thighs until cooked through
  5. Add the Hoisin Sauce and Soy Sauce
  6. Serve Immediately
